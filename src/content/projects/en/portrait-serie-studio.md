This portrait series was shot during one afternoon in the studio. The focus was on
soft side light and the calm presence of the people in front of the camera.

## Approach

I like working with a minimal setup \u2013 one light source, one backdrop colour,
and plenty of time to talk. That produces images that feel honest rather than staged.
